Campus Life

Playing Field

Established in 1925

As well as a sport and PE venue, the Tanglin Playing Field is home to many recreational events, such as Lion Dances during Chinese New Year. A student favourite, the Astro is one of the most crowded places during break times and lunch! Here many sports events happen as well: football, cricket, rugby matches and many others take place in the Playing Field.



Senior Library

Established in 1930

The Senior Library is a vast area dedicated to helping our students learn and grow. Consisting of three seperate zones, the Interactive Zone, the Study Zone, and the Collaborative Zone, the students have a wide varitey of resources to help them with their academic needs. Many students also come here during lunch for a nice and quiet place to relax, or to partake in the many ongoing activities.



Senior Canteen

Established in 1927

The Senior Canteen provides our students with snacks and lucnhes during break times. A nice environment with hundreds of seats, the canteen is a popular place where students go during lunch and break. In the canteen students can pick what meals and snacks they would like, from Western to Asian lunches, or the many snacks available at the snack bar.



Junior and Infants Sport Hall

Established in 1999

The Junior and Infants Sport Hall is one of the many sports hall we have here at Tanglin. Some of the others would be the Berrick, Griffith-Jones, Smithson, Chandarana Sports Halls, each of which house important equipment for multiple differenet sports, from gymnastiques to badminton and basketball. Competeitons and Tournaments frequently take place in these halls.



Tanglin Pool

Established in 1935

The Tanglin Pool is an important part of the campus as swimming is a big thing in the school. The Tanglin Swimming team, known as the "Merlions" consist of some of the most talented young swimmers. However not only the Merlions use the pool, it is also a PE venue. Every Tanglin Student has swimming as one of their PE topics every year. We believe that swimming is an important skill for our students to learn.
